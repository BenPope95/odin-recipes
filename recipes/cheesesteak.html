Philly Cheese Steak

Classic philly cheese steak recipe with authentic flavors that will transport your tastebuds straight to Philadelphia!

Ingredients

  • 10 to 12 ounces of ribeye steak
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 medium sweet onion, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper sliced
  • 3 slices of provolone cheese
  • 2 hoagie rolls, toasted
  • 1/4 cup cheese whiz warmed

Directions

  1. Place the steak in the freezer for 10 minutes. Trim fat from edges and slice steak into thin strips. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil into a large skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onions and peppers and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occationally until the onions and peppers start to carmelize around the edges. (around 12-15 minutes)
  3. Add remaining tablespoon of olive oil to another large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced steak spread out evenly covering as much surface area as possible. Cook for a couple minutes and then stir. It should finish cooking very quickly.
  4. Turn the heat on the steak down to low. Add the peppers and onions and then stir. Add the slices of provolone to the top of the mixture and let it melt. Once the cheese melts, stir again.
  5. Evenley scoop the cheesesteak mixture into the toasted hoagie rolls. For maximum cheesiness, drizzle the warm cheese whiz over the top and enjoy!
