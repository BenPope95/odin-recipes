Homemade Mac and Cheese

This Homemade Mac and Cheese recipe is sure to become a favorite for family dinners and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 3 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 pound elbow macoroni
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups grated sharp cheddar
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and butter the inside of baking dish
  2. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil in a large saucepan.
  3. Add the salt and macaroni and bring back to boil until pasta is finished cooking (about 12 minutes) then drain
  4. Melt butter in a 1 quart saucepan. Use a whisk to stir in flour and salt over medium heat until the roux bubbles and the flour turns pale brown (about 3 minutes)
  5. Slowly whisk in 1 cup of milk and then whisk in the remaining 1 cup. Continue to cook and stir until sauce thickens.
  6. Add the cheese and then stir until it melts. Once the cheese is melted add the drained macaroni and mix thouroughly.
  7. In a small bowl, stir the bread
  8. Transfer the macaroni and cheese to a baking dish and add buttered breadcrumbs on top
  9. Bake until the dish bubbles around the edges (about 15 minutes)
